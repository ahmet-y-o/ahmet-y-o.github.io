What is Direct Trade?

Direct Trade is a form of practice which aims to lessen the amount of intermediaries between suppliers -farmers- and roasters -us-. Without any intermediaries, in a way that both of us (farmers and roasters) become dependent on each other´s success, ethics, and professionalism. Direct Trade is more than Fair Trade as it does not depend on grants or fines, but rather on really sharing the value created by the products sold by the company.
